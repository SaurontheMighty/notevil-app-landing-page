## Worldwide Stats & Game Center Tag Display
The app displays the total number of games played worldwide and the Game Center tag of the player who has played the most games in the Settings tab. By opting in to share your Game Center tag, you agree that your tag may be publicly visible to all users of the app. You may opt out at any time in the app's Settings. Ashish Selvaraj reserves the right to remove any Game Center tag from display at any time without notice.
